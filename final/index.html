Local Businesses

Redd's Ace Hardware

Redd's Ace Hardware first opened in 1909 as the "Grayson Co-op". It was the original general store in town, established by the founding settlers of Blanding which was then known as "Grayson Utah. The Redd's have owned and operated this business for over 110 years.

San Juan Pharmacy & Gift

Located at 65 S. Main, San Juan Pharmacy in Blanding offers professional service with friendly hometown charm. In addition to finding all of your pharmaceutical needs, you will love the variety of quality gifts, and unique decorative items available.

Website

San Juan Theater

San Juan County Utah's only movie theater is a great source of entertainment. Movies show nightly, Monday through Saturday at 7:00 PM. The theater is also available for private parties. While there is only one screen and seating for 290 people, the customer service is excellent and so is the popcorn!
