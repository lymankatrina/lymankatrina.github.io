Blanding History

Blanding Utah is a rural community located in the South East corner of the State of Utah. The town of Blanding was settled by early members of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints who travelled by wagon from Escalante Utah accross a trail known as the Hole in the Rock trail. Many of Blanding's residents are directly descended from those early settlers. The citizens love the area and their roots.
