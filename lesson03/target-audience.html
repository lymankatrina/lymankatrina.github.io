Target Audience

This website will serve local business owners who cater to the needs of outdoor enthusiasts and tourists visiting the area. The target audience is people who enjoy to travel for the purpose of visiting local historical sites and attractions, and people who enjoy outdoor recreation. This includes tourists and local residents. Individuals who live in or travel to the area will be interested in this site for weather conditions, current events, significant news updates, recommendations of places to visit, and interesting facts.

Persona 1

This person is a history lover. She is a 60 year old female who has a college degree and is retired. She and her husband love to travel and visit historical places around the world, but especially around the United States. She will be interested in finding information on historical places, scenic byways, and shopping at small boutiques and antique shops.

Persona 2

This person is an outdoor enthusiast. He is a 30 year old male with an upper level degree. He loves to explore the outdoors hiking, biking, watersports, snow sports, hunting and fishing. He will be interested in finding directions and tips for enjoying the backcountry.

Scenarios

  • Visitors can find a list of places that sale antiques, gifts, and souvenier items
  • Visitors can find a list of restaurants, diners, and cafes in the area.
  • Visitors can find a list of outdoor recreation areas and the types of activites permitted in each location
  • Visitors can find a list of historical sites and places of interest in the area.
  • Visitors can find what the weather conditions will be at the recreation site they plan to visit
