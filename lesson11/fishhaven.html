Fishing for the Family

by Katrina Lyman

One of my favorite childhood memories is of my dad teaching me to fish. We didn't fish often. My dad was not a pro, and we never used fancy lures, flies, or spinners. It was usually a can of worms and sometimes a small marshmallow or ball of fish bait from a jar.

I'm sure my dad spent most of his time untangling our lines, or helping us bait our hooks, but it wasn't the fish he cared so much about, it was us, and spending time with us. You can catch a lot of good memories and maybe some great fish stories when you take your family on a fishing trip.

After we fished the morning away, we would prepare the fish and fry them over the camp-fire. That is the best way to eat trout!

There are several great lakes for fishing in Idaho, and one of the best lakes is situated beside Fish Haven. No wonder the motto is "Fish Heaven". For more information about fishing in Idaho, visit the Idaho Department of Fish and Game.

Something for Everyone

In Fish Haven there is always something to do.

Whether you enjoy biking, four-wheeling, hiking, or going for a drive, there are plenty of ways to take in the surroundings of Fish Haven.

Spend the day on Bear Lake, or stay in one of the many cabin rentals or camp areas for Fishing, boating, water-skiing, swimming, bird watching, or relaxing on the beach.
